RED WINE

Weninger Cabernet Franc, Gere winery

Full bodied Cabernet Franc with deep ruby colour. The smell is a nice addition of black currant, blackberry and coffee. Dry, high acids and complex taste. Goes well with venison, heavy beef steaks and dark chocolate desserts.

Villányi Portugieser 2013, Teleki winery

The wine’s velvety character and fine tennis provide an exciting experience. Recommended with game dishes, cheese and Hungarian goulash.

Kékfrankos Selection, Bock winery

Kékfrankos is a wine that can be found in practically every Hungarian wine region. The 2012 Selection won the Decanter World Wine Awards. It is without a doubt a premium wine. It is characteristically crimson in colour. Morello and sweet cherries can be found in its bouquet and taste, complemented by chocolate aromas.

Cabernet Sauvignon 2012, Istvándy winery

In this dry red we can notice cocoa, coffee, dried sour cherry and blueberry accompanied by a mild aroma of herbs. The intense taste shows gentle, stroking tennis and delicate use of wood. The taste and smell show all the characteristics of this terroir.

III 2011, Figula winery

A three-variety blend from Cabernet Franc, Cabernet Sauvignon and Merlot - the classical Bordeaux ingredients of this light red wine from the northern shores of Lake Balaton. Colour of ruby and flavours of cherries, plums and red currants. Always a delight to taste it.

Szekszárdi Merlot, Bodri winery

Merlot is a widely known wine type from Bordeaux, which found the ideal environment in the Szekszárd wine region. Medium bodied with soft tones, velvety texture with an addition of vanilla and black currant in taste. It goes well with marine fishes, vegetable dishes and fresh cheese.

Madárlátta Soproni Merlot, Luka winery

Elegant Merlot with deep ruby colour. The wine’s smell consists of spices and blueberries. Dry wine and more complex than average taste. Goes well with paprika spiced dishes, stews, duck and rabbit.

Bolyki Egri Bikavér

Being the signature wine of the region this one is taking its job seriously. Easily drinkable and complex at the same time. Deep crimson coloured with the smell of sour cherry and spices, dry wine with intense acids, tannin, full body and complex flavour.
