WHITE WINE

Irsai Olivér 2013, Cézár winery

Light green colour, fresh in scent, with a hint of flowery fields. The taste consists of tropical fruits and orange flower. Nice sugar - acid balance.

Chardonnay Villány, Sauska winery

The new-world version of the grand Chardonnay. Rather intense fragrance with mature citrus, peach, vanilla and coconut. Dry wine with high alcohol levels, full body and complex taste. Recommended with spicy dishes, grilled fishes, hamburgers and truffle.

Rajnai Rizling Válogatás, Pannonhalmi Apátság

One of the nicest Hungarian Rajnai. Moderate lemon colour, medium smell intensity of citrus, delicate spices and minerals. Dry wine with full acids, medium body and alcohol and complex taste.

Somlói Furmint 2011, Györgykovács winery

Fully, traditional barrel fermented and aged white harvested from a young vineyard. Lovely bouquet of rich fruitness with a palate fully of delicate acids and fine aromas of Somló.

Nagy-Somlói Zenit 2012, Tornai winery

A real Hungarian variety. Pale yellow colour with green reflexes. The fragrances of citrus are in perfect harmony with the volcanic character of Somló-hill. Vibrant and refreshing with fragrances of fresh fruits and minerals of the terroir.

Juhfark 2008, Kreinbacher winery

It is a typical grape from Somló-hill, a real Hungarian variety. This wine has a slight oaky scent mixed with ripe and dried fruits. It is a pleasure to drink - a big wine with minerals, complexity and structure. It gives more experience to the consumer than any other ‘Juhfark’.

Chardonnay 2007, Benedek winery

The smell is clear, has a hint of apple, not too intense. Vivid acids, lively with a stronger alcohol taste.

Badacsonyi Kéknyelű 2007, Szeremley winery

The oldest and noblest grape variety in Badacsony, unique and exclusive not only in Hungary but in the whole world. It is dioecious, which means it bears less fruits, but gives more concentrated wine. Despite it’s mildness it has a surprisingly long aftertaste. It’s young, fresh and rich in minerals, which makes it an ideal pairing with trout, sole and noble fishes, lobster and goat cheese. Great choice for special occasions.

Badacsonyi Szürkebarát 2011, Szeremley winery

A classic, traditional Pinot Gris of Badacsony with lively, crispy acids. Lovely vanilla notes due to ageing in a wooden barrel blend with a floral bouquet and ripe apple notes. The salty, mineral notes typical for the region prevail on the palate.
