ROSÉ

Kékfrankos Rozé, Dúzsi Tamás winery

Dúzsi is particularly good in rose making, his signature wine, Kékfrankos Rozé is medium salmon coloured, elegant with not aggressively strong peach and strawberry scent. Dry wine with rather intense acids, delicate fizziness. Recommended with lighter fish soups, sweet potato, garlic seasoning and creamy meals.

Dörgicsei Rozé 2013, Pántlika winery

A rose wine with bright pink colour. Its bouquet is dominated by strawberry and raspberry. Its lively, refreshing acids are in perfect harmony. Its fine fragrances of red berries are quite expressive, while the aftertaste offers long-lasting and concentrated aromas.

Rozé, Pannonhalmi Apátság

Fruitbomb with nice acids. Salmon coloured with the scent of raspberry and strawberry. Ideal with cooked and fried vegetables, gazpacho and pasta dishes.
